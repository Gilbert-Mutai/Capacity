ENERGY

Clean water and Energy

We are keen about ensuring universal access to affordable, reliable and modern energy and utilities by 2030. We provide a premium metering service to landlords and utility consumers of electricity, water and gas.

Loss, vandalism and wastage of energy and utilities is the biggest problem that results in expensive and rationed access, if any, to electricity, water and gas. Through our smart metering services, we are able to mitigate loss and wastages on our system and employ early detection procedures to minimize loss. Our system is able to account for every unit of water or energy, giving remote logs real time and a pay-as-you-go model of revenue collection.

Consumers are able to analyze and track their consumption real time with access to actionable insights geared towards helping them reduce their overall bill and be conscious about the effects of their unsustainable consumption.

We are all about conservation, sustainability and green initiatives.

We have crafted unique affordable models for consumers to adopt green renewable energy. Through our brand, Solar Electric, we have powered over 4000 households with solar and wind energy. Our best performing “lease-to-own” model has encouraged a higher uptake of renewable energy as the capital expenditure is spread across an extended time period making the infrastructure easy and faster to adopt.

We have partnered with stakeholders in the government and private sector to push for policies that will see the expansion and upgrading of energy services and infrastructure giving the consumer price options and autonomy in their choice of utility provider.
