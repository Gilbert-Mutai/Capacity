CONSTRUCTION

Modern and Affordable Housing

We believe that for enhanced productivity at construction sites, modern construction technology solutions and IT services increase efficiency and cost savings. Due to the surge in urban population, the need and demand for housing options has also skyrocketed. To achieve this, industry experts rely on technology to automate, design and also manage the construction process

Our solutions rely on data to help in optimizing the construction process. We provide a no-code platform that populates surveys, geolocation, design of 2D and 3D models, architectural design plans with certified bills of quantities based on inputs such as size of land, plinth area or house size, type of house, roof etc. We have partnered with registered architects, planners, surveyors and contractors to provide certified designs that are registrable and implementable.

The resultant impact on the society is huge cost savings, convenience, better planning and development and also optimal management of resources during and upon project completion. This leads to a higher client satisfaction, better employment opportunities and also better management of resources leading to a cost-effective and time efficient construction process.
