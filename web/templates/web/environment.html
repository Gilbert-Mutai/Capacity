ENVIRONMENT

Waste Management & Environmental Sustainability

The problem of waste accumulation and its impact on the environment has been a growing concern over the years. In different parts of the world, this menace has caused grievous implications on health, population and also the economic and living standards. Having a data-driven approach is pertinent in waste management across the world. Use of technology in environment management could take place in different ways to achieve sustainable waste management.

Technology makes it possible to have an efficient and effective way of managing waste. Our solutions employ artificial intelligence and block chain technology to address this problem of waste management. We have widely employed automatic waste segregation and deployment of sensor approaches for waste management in the region. The solutions ranging from smart bins, conversion and recycling through innovative approaches has led to better utilization of natural resources, efficient and effective alternative forms of energy and also creation of job opportunities.
